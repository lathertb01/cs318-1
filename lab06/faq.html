What is Haymarket Landing?

Haymarket Landing is a unique opportunity for UWEC students to be part of the dynamic revitalization of downtown Eau Claire. This six story brand new building will offer first floor retail spaces and five floors of livable space. Along with an adjacent community arts center, the Eau Claire and Chippewa rivers and Phoenix Park, the Confluence Project is a major cornerstone of community development.
