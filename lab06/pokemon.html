Madam Azumarill

Madam Azumarill (Japanese: マスター マリルリ Master Marilli) is a Pokémon in the Magical Pokémon Journey series. She is Apricot's and Marimaru's teacher in the ninja arts and is even more talented than Master Ditto is in the ninja arts. She is Marimaru's mother.

Clefairy

Clefairy (Japanese: ピッピ Pippi) is a main character in the manga series Magical Pokémon Journey and its sequel, Pokémon Chamo-Chamo ☆ Pretty ♪. He is the second Pokémon befriended by Hazel, first appearing in Clefairy Comes Through!. Like many Pokémon in the manga, he is able to speak human language

Squirtle

Squirtle (Japanese: ゼニガメ Zenigame) is a character in the Magical Pokémon Journey manga. He lives at Wigglytuff and Jigglypuff's mansion and is apparently their butler. He can speak human language. Squirtle has five sisters—two Blastoise named Kameko and Kamemi, a Wartortle named Kamena, and two younger Squirtle named Kameyo and Kamee.

Pikachu

Pikachu (Japanese: ピカチュウ) is a main character in the manga series Magical Pokémon Journey and its sequel, Pokémon Chamo-Chamo ☆ Pretty

Master Ditto

Master Ditto (Japanese: 師匠メタモン Master Metamon) is a master ninja who is also a Pokémon. He appears several times in Magical Pokémon Journey.

Enta Ninetales

Enta's Ninetales (Japanese: エンタのキュウコン Enta's Kyukon) is a Pokémon from the Pokémon Battle Frontier manga. It is Enta's first Pokémon who he has a strong bond with.
